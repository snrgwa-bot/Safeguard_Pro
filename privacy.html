Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 13, 2026

Essential Dev Team ("we", "us", "our") operates the SafeGuard mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Data TypePurpose
Email addressAccount creation, authentication, and account recovery
Account passwordSecurely stored (hashed) for authentication
Contact names, email addresses, phone numbers, and relationship labelsDelivering legacy messages and SOS alerts to your designated recipients
Legacy message contentStoring and delivering your messages when triggered
Distress message contentStoring the message sent to your contacts when a distress PIN or SOS is triggered
Deadman-switch configurationStoring your check-in schedule, deadlines, and trigger settings to operate the deadman-switch feature
Check-in timestampsMonitoring your check-in activity to trigger message delivery if a deadline is missed
GPS coordinates (optional)Including your location in SOS emergency alerts, only when you enable this feature
Security event logsRecording failed login attempts and SOS events for your account security
Subscription statusManaging free vs. premium feature access

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the Service:

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To store and deliver your legacy messages to your designated contacts
  • To send SOS emergency alerts to your emergency contacts
  • To monitor check-in deadlines and trigger message delivery when appropriate
  • To manage your subscription and provide access to the correct features
  • To log security events for your protection

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — Database hosting, user authentication, and serverless functions. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Resend — Email delivery service used to send legacy messages and SOS alerts. Resend Privacy Policy
  • Twilio — SMS delivery service. SOS alerts and scheduled messages may be delivered by SMS through Twilio; your emergency contacts' phone numbers are transmitted to Twilio to deliver those messages. Twilio Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store — Subscription billing and payment processing. Apple Privacy Policy

These services process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Account passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Your app PIN and distress PIN are stored locally on your device.
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data from our servers within 30 days.
  • Security logs may be retained for up to 90 days for fraud prevention.
  • Once legacy messages are sent via email, we have no control over copies held by recipients or their email providers.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at essentialdevteam@gmail.com.

7. Location Data

GPS location data is only collected when:

  • You have explicitly enabled location sharing in the App settings.
  • An SOS distress alert is triggered.

Location data is transmitted only to your designated emergency contacts via the SOS email. We do not track your location continuously or use it for any purpose beyond the SOS feature.

8. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions. Each is optional and used only for the stated feature:

  • Location — Attached to SOS alerts, only when you enable location sharing.
  • Contacts — Used when you choose to import emergency contacts from your address book.
  • Camera, Microphone, and Photo Library — Used when you choose to add photo, video, or audio attachments to your messages.
  • Notifications — Used to remind you about upcoming check-in deadlines.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

9. Children's Privacy

SafeGuard is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
