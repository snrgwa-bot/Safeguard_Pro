Silent Distress Feature

If you're ever in danger, enter your secret distress PIN instead of your normal PIN. The app opens normally — but silently sends an SOS email with your location to your emergency contacts. No one around you will know.

Important: SafeGuard is not an emergency service. Always call 911 first if you are in immediate danger.
