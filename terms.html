1. Overview of the Service

SafeGuard is a personal communication tool that allows you to:

  • Create pre-written legacy messages and designate contacts to receive them.
  • Set a check-in timer so that if you fail to check in within your chosen interval, your pre-written messages are emailed to your designated contacts.
  • Use a Silent Distress feature that, when activated via a secret PIN, silently sends a customizable SOS email (optionally including your GPS location) to your chosen emergency contacts.

The App facilitates email-based notifications to contacts you designate. It does not interact with, replace, or connect to any government emergency service, law enforcement agency, or first responder system.
